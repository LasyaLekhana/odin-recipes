Hot Cocoa

Description

Cozy up with a big mug of hot chocolate this winter. This classic hot chocolate recipe combines simple ingredients to make an incredibly rich and creamy, comforting drink. Smooth, chocolaty, and oh-so satisfying, this cold weather favorite is perfect for chilly evenings, snowy afternoons, or anytime you want a homemade treat to warm you up.

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup white sugar, or to taste
  • ⅓ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 pinch salt
  • ⅓ cup boiling water
  • 3 ½ cups milk
  • ¾ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ cup half-and-half cream

Directions

  1. Step 1

    Gather the ingredients.

  2. Step 2

    Combine sugar, cocoa powder, and salt in a saucepan; add boiling water and whisk until smooth. Bring mixture to a simmer, stirring continuously to prevent scorching, and cook until slightly thickened, about 2 minutes.

  3. Step 3

    Stir in milk and heat until very hot without boiling.

  4. Step 4

    Remove from heat; add vanilla.

  5. Step 5

    Add cream to each mug to help cool cocoa to drinking temperature, then divide hot cocoa between 4 mugs.

  6. Step 6

    Serve hot and enjoy!
